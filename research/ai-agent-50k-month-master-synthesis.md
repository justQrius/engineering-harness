## The Universal Pricing Sweet Spot

**Across all 6 verticals: $99-299/month is the consensus sweet spot for SMBs/solos**

### Why $99-299/mo Works
- Low enough for founder to swipe personal card without approval
- High enough to signal real value ($100+/mo = "this matters")
- Matches "saves $500-2K/month" ROI story — 3-5x return
- Below the "need to get budget approval" threshold
- Self-serve SAAS model — no sales cycle needed

| Vertical | Documented Setup Fee Range | What Works |
